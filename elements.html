About Me

Hello!

Hello! I’m passionate about understanding people and using data to improve the way we work. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and I’m currently completing a Master’s in Data Science. My goal is to combine these disciplines to specialize in Industrial-Organizational (I/O) Psychology.

I enjoy analyzing patterns in human behavior and using those insights to help organizations make better, more ethical decisions. Whether it’s through survey analysis, organizational research, or workforce analytics, I’m driven by the impact data can have on workplace well-being and performance.

Blending Psychology and Data

With a foundation in behavioral science and training in machine learning, I’m excited to help teams solve complex challenges—from hiring and engagement to performance and retention—using evidence-based approaches.
